About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the ECE Department at the University of Southern California, and I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Peter A. Beerel.

My research interests include
  • ML: SAL-ViT, C2PI, RNA-ViT
  • Hardware security: TriLock, Fun-SAT
My research vision aims to provide a comprehensive and vertically integrated suite of solutions for data privacy—from the algorithmic core to the silicon substrate—ensuring that privacy is not just an add-on feature but a foundational principle of modern ML systems.
